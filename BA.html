Higher National Diploma In Business Administration - (HNDBA)


Entry Profile

Applicants should have one of the followings:

GCE (A/L) Examination (old syllabus) in Economics, Commerce & Finance and Accountancy or GCE (A/L) Examination in any two of the above three subjects and a pass in one of the following subjects: Political Science, Pure Mathematics, Geography, Sinhala, Tamil, English, Logic, History, Business Statistic at the same examination.

OR

GCE (A/L) Examination (new syllabus) in Economics, Business Studies and Accountancy or GCE (A/L) Examination in any two of the above three subjects and a pass in Geography, Home Economics, Political Science, Logic & Scientific Methods, Agricultural Science, Combined Mathematics and Business Statistic at the same examination

OR

Successfull completion of National Certificate in Accounting Technicians conducted by the Department of Technical Education

AND

An Ordinary passes in English and Mathematics at GCE (O/L)
